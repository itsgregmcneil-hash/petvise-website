For Veterinary Practices

PetVise is developing a subscription-based teletriage service to support veterinary practices in the Raleigh–Triangle area. Our goal is to help your clients get reliable guidance before they arrive, while reinforcing trust in your team.

What We Offer

  • After-hours triage support for your existing clients
  • Hospital matching to connect pet parents with the right care
  • Help setting expectations about wait times and diagnostics
  • Clear documentation your team can reference in-house
  • Seamless integration with your practice workflow
